Silesia War Bot

Silesia War Bot is war simulation inspired by World War Bot. There are 36 counties fighting for the hegemony over Silesia. Important factor of the victory is county score, based on data from 2018:

  • population
  • healthcare (hospital beds per 10k inhabitants and inhabitants per 1 healthcare entity)
  • readership (book loans per inhabitant)
  • net income (based on gross income and expenses)

To find initial score all features were scaled to values from 0 to 1, then the average of them was taken. During the war a score is calculated as mean value of scores of all subordinate counties. The score is then scaled to present probability of a new attack. The higher the score is, the more likely is that the county will attack a neighbour. The game finishes when only one player lefts and the player controls all other counties.
